INTRODUCTION

At the dawn of the 26th of Rajab, 1445 of the Islamic calendar (which is on the 26th of March 2024 ), Muslims in Nigeria sojourn the earth deprived of security, land, personality, food and shelter, life and other things guaranteed them by Allah to lead an exemplary life of leaders and best of mankind. This condition was brought about by the thoroughly bastardised, alien environment they were made to live in and eventual corruption of the soul that ought to be pure in faith.

The idea of setting up an organization for Muslim students was born out of Muslims’ desires to safeguard their religious fundamentals, culture, and identity from the twin effects of forceful evangelization and alluring Christian activities.

TAWHEED

01

In Islam is the basic principle in our vision because it's the natural and universal answer to the spiritual dimension of the human being in understanding himself in this life and the hereafter. It is also the ceiling of the human logic in understanding the dimensions of life and existence and what is beyond. Realizing tawheed gives the human humbleness so that he can take his life and his succession role in this life seriously to achieve what he was created for and rest assured about the hereafter.

SUCCESSION

02

The principle of succession, simply put, we are here to make the world a better place for everyone "And, when your Lord said to the angels, indeed, I will make upon the earth a successive authority, it is not a Quranic mandate that does not have a basis in the human nature, but it's an expression of the human being qualities and abilities that made him enjoys awareness and perception more than any other creature in his world Everything in this universe was created and subjected for us so that we realize the reason we were created for, to build a better world.
