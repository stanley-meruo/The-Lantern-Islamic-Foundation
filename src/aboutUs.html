TAWHEED

01

In Islam is the basic principle in our vision because it's the natural and universal answer to the spiritual dimension of the human being in understanding himself in this life and the hereafter. It is also the ceiling of the human logic in understanding the dimensions of life and existence and what is beyond. Realizing tawheed gives the human humbleness so that he can take his life and his succession role in this life seriously to achieve what he was created for and rest assured about the hereafter.

SUCCESSION
