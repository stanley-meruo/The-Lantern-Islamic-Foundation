FIVE PILLARS OF ISLAM

S A W N
Sawn is the act of fasting during the holy month of Ramadam. Muslims are required to fast, which entails abstaining from, drink sexual relations and displeasing speech and behaviour from dawn until dusk.
S A L A H
Salah is the ritual required of every muslim five times a day thoughout their lifetime.
Z A K A R
Zakar or Almsgiving is the act of giving a portion of a muslim's wealth to those in need throughout their lifetime. This. means that Zakat is mandatory for Muslims, along with the four sacred pillars of Islam.
S H A H A D A H
Shahadah as a pillar of Islam is ths declaration of faith in one God(Allah) and his messenger (peace be upon him)
H A J J
Hajj is the sacred pilgrimage to Mecca required of every Muslim at least once in their lifetime if it is within their means and reach. Hajj takes place during the same period each year.
